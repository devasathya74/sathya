Here is a sample application for sick leave to the principal:

[Your Name]
[Your Class/Grade]
[Your Roll Number]
[Date]

The Principal,
[School/College Name]
[Address]

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to request a leave of absence from school for [number] days, starting from [date] to [date], as I am currently unwell and unable to attend classes.

Unfortunately, I am suffering from [briefly mention your illness, e.g. fever, flu, stomach infection, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and avoid any physical activity for the next [number] days to ensure a speedy recovery.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will also provide a medical certificate from my doctor, if required.

I would be grateful if you could grant me leave for the aforementioned period. Please let me know if there are any concerns or if there is anything else I can provide to support my application.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that you should customize this letter according to your needs and circumstances. Also, be sure to keep a polite and respectful tone throughout the letter.
